Clarity & Balance

For skin seeking peace

Acne can be frustrating, stubborn, and deeply personal. At Esteem, we understand—and we're here to help. Through advanced technology and compassionate expertise, we address acne at its source, restoring your skin to its naturally clear, confident state. Every treatment journey begins with understanding the root cause of your breakouts.

Skin Plasma Treatment

Advanced Bacterial Elimination

Our most advanced acne solution. This cutting-edge plasma technology sterilizes bacteria on contact, regulates oil production, and stimulates healing—all with zero downtime. Clinical evidence shows remarkable results for active, persistent acne.

Skin Plasma uses new, advanced plasma technology that creates a sterile environment on the skin's surface, removing acne-causing bacteria while simultaneously boosting collagen production. It can reduce sebum production, improve skin tightness, and increase dermal density—addressing multiple acne factors at once.

Ideal for: Active breakouts, bacterial acne, oil control, persistent acne

Royal Cleo Multi-Modality

Comprehensive Acne Care

A comprehensive approach combining cupping, microdermabrasion, micro-channeling, microcurrent, and LED therapy to address congestion, scarring, and texture. This technology is safe and effective for many skin types with no pain or downtime.

Royal Cleo has been shown to improve congested oily skin, scars, stretch marks, dehydration, fine lines and wrinkles, pigmentation, sun spots, and uneven skin tone. It's particularly effective for acne because it addresses multiple contributing factors in a single treatment.

Ideal for: Congested skin, acne scars, uneven texture, oily skin

Meta Therapy Microneedling

Natural Skin Improvement

100% natural skin renewal through precision needling. Meta Therapy conditions the skin's surface using specially designed modules that remove dead cells and deeply cleanse, resulting in a more luminous and glowing appearance.

This treatment helps minimize acne scarring, reduces hyperpigmentation, and improves overall texture. By creating controlled micro-channels, it allows deeper product penetration and triggers the skin's natural healing response—all without harsh chemicals or downtime.

Ideal for: Acne scars, texture refinement, deep cleansing, hyperpigmentation

LHE Phototherapy System

Light & Heat Energy

The powerful science of the most advanced acne treatment is now available at Esteem. Long used by dermatologists and skincare professionals, LHE (Light & Heat Energy) is a safe and clinically proven alternative to drugs.

It uses light to kill acne-causing bacteria and heat to bring down inflammation in the skin and glands. The benefits? All-natural with no need for medication, safe with no UV and no side effects, fast and easy with quick flash-and-go sequences, and it heals inflammation so skin looks and feels better immediately.

Ideal for: Inflammatory acne, sensitive skin, medication-free treatment

Celluma LED Light Therapy

NASA-Pioneered Healing

Pioneered by NASA and now accepted as a natural, non-invasive way to treat acne, anti-aging, and pain management. The application of biophotonic light therapy encourages natural cellular activity through light-emitting diodes applied to the skin's surface.

LLLT normalizes cellular function, providing relief from inflammation. Celluma triggers a cascade of metabolic processes which restores compromised cellular function, creating a path to revitalized, clear skin. Blue light kills acne bacteria while red light reduces inflammation and promotes healing.

Ideal for: Active acne, inflammation, healing, natural treatment

Back Acne Treatment

Beyond the Face

Annoying acne isn't limited to your face. This clinical treatment combines fruit enzymes, beta hydroxy acids, and powerful anti-inflammatory botanicals to eliminate stubborn back acne (often called "bacne").

The treatment deeply exfoliates, unclogs pores, and reduces inflammation while being gentle enough for sensitive skin. Regular treatments combined with proper home care can dramatically improve back acne and prevent future breakouts.

Ideal for: Body acne, back breakouts, chest acne, shoulder acne

Microdermabrasion

The Lunch Time Peel

This treatment delivers immediate results with an average procedure time of less than 30 minutes. Also known as the "Lunch Time Peel" because you can return to work or other daily activities immediately—no recovery or healing time needed.

Microdermabrasion removes the outer layer of dead skin cells and debris, deeply cleanses congested pores, and promotes cellular turnover. It's particularly effective for acne-prone skin because it helps prevent pore blockages that lead to breakouts.

Ideal for: Clogged pores, blackheads, dull skin, prevention

Oxygen Enriched Facial

Germicidal Clarity

This treatment can be used on any skin type. The germicidal action of pure oxygen makes it extremely beneficial for acne, while the lightening and vasoconstricting capabilities deliver instant results for clearer, brighter skin with a feeling of firmness.

Purified oxygen and negative ions are infused into the skin, fighting bacteria while boosting product absorption. The result is immediate clarity and a calming effect on inflamed, acne-prone skin. Add the Cocoa Enzyme Peel for enhanced results.

Ideal for: Active breakouts, brightening, calming, all skin types
