Policies & Guidelines

Important information to ensure the best experience

Appointment Guidelines

At Esteem, your time is valued—and so is ours. To ensure we can provide the best possible experience for all our clients, we kindly request your cooperation with the following policies.

Scheduling

All appointments are by reservation only. We recommend booking in advance, as our schedule fills quickly. You may schedule your appointment by:

  • Calling or texting 405.850.7122
  • Emailing esteem@cox.net
  • Booking through our online system (if available)

First-Time Clients

We begin every new client relationship with a complimentary consultation. This allows us to assess your skin, understand your goals, and design a personalized treatment plan. Please arrive 10 minutes early to complete intake forms.

New clients receive a complimentary skin consultation with their first treatment.

Cancellations & Rescheduling

We understand that life unfolds unexpectedly. If you need to adjust your appointment, please provide 24 hours notice. This allows us to offer your reserved time to clients on our waiting list.

Cancellation Policy

Your appointment time is reserved exclusively for you. When appointments are canceled with less than 24 hours notice, we miss the opportunity to serve other clients who are waiting for care.

  • 24+ hours notice: No charge, reschedule at your convenience
  • Less than 24 hours: Full service fee applies
  • No-show appointments: Full service fee will be charged

We appreciate your understanding. These policies help us maintain the intimate, personalized service that defines the Esteem experience.

Late Arrivals

To honor the appointments scheduled after yours, late arrivals may result in shortened treatment sessions. We'll do our best to accommodate you, but cannot guarantee a full treatment if you arrive late.

  • Please arrive 5-10 minutes early for your appointment
  • Late arrivals of 15+ minutes may need to be rescheduled
  • Service fees apply even if time constraints prevent full treatment delivery

If you're running late, please call or text us immediately at 405.850.7122 so we can adjust accordingly.

Payment & Pricing

Payment Methods

We accept the following forms of payment:

  • Cash
  • All major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover)
  • Debit cards

Pricing

Treatment pricing is discussed during your consultation. We believe in transparent, upfront pricing with no hidden fees. Package pricing and series discounts are available for many treatments.

Gratuity

While gratuities are never expected, they are always appreciated and may be added to your service payment if you wish to show your appreciation.

Treatment Preparation

To ensure optimal results from your treatment, please follow these guidelines:

Before Your Appointment

  • Arrive with clean skin (no makeup for facial treatments)
  • Avoid sun exposure and tanning 72 hours before certain treatments
  • Disclose all medications, skincare products, and health conditions
  • Avoid caffeine and alcohol before body treatments
  • Stay well-hydrated in the days leading up to your appointment

After Your Treatment

  • Follow all post-treatment care instructions provided
  • Avoid sun exposure and use SPF as directed
  • Drink plenty of water to support your body's natural processes
  • Avoid harsh products or activities as recommended
  • Contact us with any questions or concerns

Specific preparation and aftercare instructions will be provided for your particular treatment during consultation.

Health & Safety

Contraindications

Certain health conditions may prevent or require modification of treatments. Please inform us if you:

  • Are pregnant or nursing
  • Have active infections, open wounds, or skin conditions
  • Are undergoing cancer treatment (we have specialized protocols)
  • Have pacemakers or other medical devices
  • Are taking medications that affect your skin
  • Have allergies to products or ingredients

Your Safety Matters

Your health and safety are our highest priority. We maintain strict sanitation protocols, use only professional-grade products, and follow all industry standards. All equipment is properly maintained and calibrated.

If you're feeling unwell on the day of your appointment, please reschedule. We want you to enjoy your treatment in comfort and good health.

Questions About Our Policies?

We're happy to discuss any of these policies in detail. Your comfort and understanding are important to us.
