The Art of Brow Shaping

Your eyebrows frame your face and define your expression. At Esteem, brow shaping is treated as an art form—we consider your facial structure, features, and personal style to create brows that enhance your natural beauty.

Brow Waxing vs. Shaping

Brow Waxing: Quick maintenance for established brow shapes. We remove stray hairs and clean up the edges, maintaining your current shape. Ideal for clients with well-defined brows who need regular upkeep.

Brow Shaping & Waxing: A comprehensive service that includes analysis, design, and precision waxing to create your ideal brow shape. We consider your bone structure, eye shape, and facial proportions. Perfect for first-time clients or those wanting to redesign their brows.

Brow Tinting

Semi-permanent dye is applied to enhance or define the natural brow color. Tinting adds depth, creates the appearance of fuller brows, and can help balance asymmetry. Results last 3-4 weeks.

Particularly beneficial for those with light or sparse brows, or anyone who wants to reduce daily brow makeup routine. We custom-match the tint to your hair color and desired intensity.

What to Expect

Before Your Appointment

Allow brows to grow for at least 2-3 weeks before shaping. Avoid tweezing or trimming. If you're getting tinting, avoid retinoids around the brow area for 48 hours prior.

During Your Service

We'll discuss your goals and preferences, then carefully shape and groom your brows. Most services take 15-30 minutes. Waxing is quick with minimal discomfort. Tinting requires 10-15 minutes of processing time.

After Care

Avoid hot showers, steam, or heavy exercise for 24 hours after waxing. Don't apply makeup or active skincare products to the waxed area for 24 hours. For tinting, avoid oil-based cleansers near brows to extend results.

Maintenance

Brow waxing: Every 3-4 weeks for optimal shape maintenance. Tinting: Refresh every 3-4 weeks as color fades. Regular maintenance ensures your brows always look polished and intentional.
