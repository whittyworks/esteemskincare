Medical-Grade Peels

From gentle renewal to dramatic transformation

Chemical peels are one of the most effective tools for skin transformation. By carefully removing damaged outer layers, we reveal fresher, healthier skin while stimulating your body's natural renewal processes. Each peel is carefully selected during your consultation to address your specific concerns with precision and care.

All peels are customized to your skin type and tolerance. We'll discuss expected downtime and results during your consultation.

Gentle · No Downtime

Enzyme Complex Peel

A gentle solution that can be incorporated into a regular skincare regimen. This organic enzyme peel works progressively to diminish age spots, fine lines, and discoloration while improving pores, skin tone, and texture.

Perfect for those new to peels or with sensitive skin, the enzyme complex provides visible results without irritation or downtime. Can be done regularly as part of maintenance care.

Ideal for: First-time peels, sensitive skin, age spots, regular maintenance

Gentle · No Downtime

Cocoa Enzyme Peel

You'll love this magnificent aromatic treatment. This enzyme peel is effective for all skin types and particularly beneficial for those suffering with acne or rosacea.

The gentle cocoa enzymes provide effective exfoliation while calming inflammation and nourishing the skin. The luxurious aroma creates a spa-like experience while the treatment works to refine and clarify.

Ideal for: Acne, rosacea, sensitive skin, all skin types, gentle exfoliation

Gentle · No Downtime

Pumpkin Peel

An organic and medically effective blend of papaya, pumpkin, and mango. This seasonal-inspired peel works to rebalance and rejuvenate tired, dull, stressed skin.

Rich in enzymes and antioxidants, the pumpkin peel gently dissolves dead skin cells while nourishing and protecting. Perfect for fall and winter when skin needs extra care and revival.

Ideal for: Dull skin, stressed skin, environmental damage, seasonal care

Moderate · Minimal Flaking

Glycolic Peel

This peel safely removes the outer layers of dead skin cells, bringing fresh new skin to the surface. Glycolic acid is particularly effective for sun spots, age spots, acne-prone skin, and blackhead-prone skin.

As one of the most studied and proven alpha hydroxy acids, glycolic delivers reliable results for texture improvement, hyperpigmentation reduction, and pore refinement.

Ideal for: Sun damage, age spots, acne-prone skin, blackheads, texture

Moderate · Minimal Flaking

Lacto-Iconic Gel Peel

This peel is milder on the skin than other peels and well-tolerated by most skin types. This lactic acid exfoliator removes the outer layer of thickened or damaged skin, leaving a smoother, healthier appearance.

Lactic acid is known for its hydrating properties while still providing effective exfoliation, making it ideal for those with drier skin types who still want peel benefits.

Ideal for: Dry skin, sensitive skin, texture, hydration, mild exfoliation

Moderate · Visible Results

Mixed Berry Polish Peel

Take your microdermabrasion to the next level with an organic blend of fruit enzymes and skin-correcting peptides. This treatment leaves your skin nourished, plump, and hydrated after exfoliation.

Unlike traditional microdermabrasion that can leave skin dry, the Mixed Berry Polish combines mechanical and enzymatic exfoliation with deep hydration. No downtime, great for all types of skin.

Ideal for: Enhanced exfoliation, hydration, all skin types, no downtime

Advanced · Visible Flaking

GetGlowing Micro-Peel

Using active ingredients, this downtime peel encourages the exfoliation of dead skin cells and debris while stimulating the skin's natural renewal process. Suitable for all skin types and Fitzpatrick scales.

Expect visible flaking for 3-5 days as the peel works to reveal dramatically improved skin texture, tone, and clarity. The results are worth the brief social downtime.

Ideal for: Dramatic results, texture correction, all skin types, committed clients

Advanced · 3-4 Days Downtime

MandeliClear Peel

This powerful peel combines the strength of a Jessner with the lightening properties of Mandelic acid to target pigmentation related to sun damage or acne. Expect 3-4 days of moderate flaking or peeling.

MandeliClear is our go-to treatment for stubborn hyperpigmentation, melasma, and post-inflammatory pigmentation from acne. The results in evening skin tone are remarkable.

Ideal for: Stubborn pigmentation, melasma, sun damage, post-acne marks

Deep · 5-7 Days Downtime

Jessner Peel

Combines multiple acids together for a stronger, deeper peel providing very noticeable and effective exfoliation. This is our most intensive peel option.

The Jessner peel penetrates deeper layers of skin, addressing significant sun damage, deep wrinkles, severe acne scarring, and major texture concerns. Expect significant peeling for up to a week, followed by dramatically renewed skin.

Ideal for: Deep resurfacing, severe damage, significant scarring, maximum results

What to Expect

Before Your Peel

Avoid sun exposure, retinoids, and certain medications as directed. We'll provide complete pre-treatment instructions during your consultation.

During Treatment

Most peels take 30-45 minutes. You may feel tingling or warmth—this is normal and indicates the peel is working. We monitor your comfort throughout.

After Your Peel

Follow post-care instructions carefully. Avoid sun exposure, use gentle products, and stay hydrated. Peeling typically begins 2-3 days after treatment and lasts 3-7 days depending on peel depth.

Results

Once peeling is complete, you'll reveal dramatically improved skin—smoother texture, even tone, reduced discoloration, and a healthy glow. Results continue to improve over the following weeks as collagen production increases.
