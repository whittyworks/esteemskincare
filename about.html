Mary Reed

Founder & Lead Clinician · LE, COE, CDP

Mary Reed didn't just create Esteem—she envisioned a sanctuary where clinical excellence meets genuine care, where every treatment is both transformative and deeply personal.

As a Licensed Esthetician with over two decades of specialized training, Mary has devoted her career to mastering the art and science of skin health. Her expertise spans advanced modalities including microneedling, chemical peels, cryogenic treatments, IPL, and the exclusive technologies that define Esteem's approach.

But Mary's journey is more than credentials. As a cancer survivor, she intimately understands the relationship between skin health and overall wellbeing. This experience led her to become a Certified Oncology Esthetician—a designation she holds with profound pride. Through this specialized training, Mary provides compassionate, expert care to clients navigating cancer treatment, adapting protocols to honor both medical needs and emotional resilience.

Mary's philosophy is simple: every client deserves to feel seen, understood, and celebrated. She approaches each consultation as a collaboration, listening deeply to your concerns and aspirations before crafting a treatment plan that's uniquely yours. Her commitment to ongoing education ensures Esteem remains at the forefront of aesthetic innovation, offering you access to the most advanced, proven techniques available.

Technology That Transforms

We don't follow trends—we set standards. Our commitment to excellence means partnering with the world's most innovative aesthetic technologies.

Paris-Born Precision

Neveskin

Imported from France, Neveskin represents the pinnacle of non-invasive body and facial contouring. Using precisely controlled thermal technology, this treatment permanently reduces fat cells, smooths cellulite, and lifts facial contours—all without surgery, downtime, or discomfort.

Results are gradual, natural, and lasting. Each session works with your body's natural systems, which is why treatments are strategically scheduled for optimal transformation.

Ideal for: Body sculpting, facial lifting, cellulite reduction

Exclusive to Oklahoma

TORC Plus

Esteem is the only clinic in Oklahoma offering TORC technology—an FDA-cleared system that builds lean muscle while you rest. Through targeted bio-electrical stimulation, TORC firms and tones abs, thighs, buttocks, and arms, complementing your fitness routine or jumpstarting your body transformation journey.

For the face, TORC microcurrent delivers our most sought-after anti-aging results: lifted contours, diminished fine lines, improved tone and texture. It's the non-surgical facelift that actually works.

Ideal for: Muscle toning, body contouring, facial lifting, anti-aging

Advanced Regeneration

Skin Plasma

This cutting-edge plasma technology works beneath the surface to stimulate collagen, sterilize acne-causing bacteria, and regenerate skin cells—all with zero downtime. Skin Plasma treats active acne, lifts sagging skin, and dramatically improves product absorption for amplified results.

Ideal for: Acne treatment, collagen building, skin tightening

Pure Infusion

Oxygen Dome

Experience the luxury of breathing pure oxygen while your skin drinks it in. The Oxygen Dome infuses negative ions and purified oxygen deep into tissue, boosting circulation, balancing pH, and maximizing the benefits of every product applied during your treatment.

The result? Luminous, revitalized skin and a profoundly relaxing experience that benefits your entire body.

Ideal for: Enhancing facial treatments, deep hydration, relaxation
