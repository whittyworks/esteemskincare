Microneedling & Micro-Channeling

Precision collagen induction for transformative results

Microneedling represents one of the most effective approaches to skin rejuvenation available today. By creating controlled micro-injuries to the skin, we trigger your body's natural healing response—resulting in fresh collagen production, improved texture, and dramatic reduction in scars and wrinkles. At Esteem, we use only medical-grade, FDA-cleared devices for safe, effective results.

SkinPen Microneedling

FDA-Cleared Collagen Induction

A minimally invasive, non-surgical procedure for facial rejuvenation that uses precision needles to create controlled micro-channels in the skin. This triggers your skin's natural healing response, producing fresh collagen and elastin along with new capillary formation.

SkinPen is the first FDA-cleared microneedling device, ensuring safety and efficacy. The treatment improves scars (including acne scars), reduces wrinkles, enhances skin firmness and hydration, and dramatically improves texture. Also highly effective for stretch marks and melasma.

Results develop progressively over weeks as new collagen forms. Most clients see optimal results after a series of 3-6 treatments spaced 4-6 weeks apart.

Ideal for: Acne scars, deep wrinkles, texture, stretch marks, rejuvenation

Meta Therapy Microneedling

100% Natural Skin Improvement

Meta Therapy conditions the skin's surface using specially designed polycarbonate modules that remove dead skin cells and deeply cleanse the skin, resulting in a more luminous and glowing appearance.

This treatment helps minimize scarring, wrinkles, hyperpigmentation, and overall texture concerns. By creating micro-channels, Meta Therapy allows for deeper product penetration while stimulating collagen production—all through 100% natural mechanical action.

Understanding Microneedling

How It Works

Microneedling creates thousands of micro-channels in the skin, triggering three phases of healing: inflammation (which kicks off the healing process), proliferation (where new collagen and elastin form), and remodeling (where the skin continues to improve for months). This is your body's own regenerative power, simply activated by controlled injury.

What to Expect

During treatment: Topical numbing ensures comfort. Most clients describe the sensation as light pressure or vibration. Treatment takes 30-60 minutes depending on area.

Immediately after: Skin will be red and slightly swollen, similar to a sunburn. This typically resolves within 24-48 hours.

Recovery: Minimal downtime. Most clients return to normal activities the next day. Avoid sun exposure and use gentle products as directed.

Results Timeline

Initial improvements (glow, texture) appear within a week. Collagen production continues for 3-6 months after treatment. Optimal results typically achieved with a series of 3-6 treatments spaced 4-6 weeks apart. Results are cumulative and long-lasting.

Best Candidates

Microneedling is suitable for most skin types and tones. Ideal for those with acne scars, wrinkles, texture concerns, stretch marks, or anyone seeking overall skin rejuvenation. During consultation, we'll determine if microneedling is right for you and create a personalized treatment plan.
